Where to find these details on your driving licence

Enter the information from your driving licence using the numbered labels to help you.

  • surname is labelled ‘1’
  • issue date is ‘4a’
  • expiry date is ‘4b’
  • licence number is ‘5’
  • issue number is the two digits after the licence number, and may be separated by a space or on a separate line
