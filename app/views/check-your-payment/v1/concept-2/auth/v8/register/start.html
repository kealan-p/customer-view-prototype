Before you start

You will need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone

You will also need information from 2 sources so we can check we are dealing with the right person. You can choose from:

  • a UK passport
  • payslips or P60
  • Self Assessment
  • information about tax credits, if you claim them
  • details of your bank accounts, loans, mortgages or credit agreements

What happens next

We will send security codes to your email and mobile phone to check that they belong to you.

You will create a password, which you will use with your email address to sign in securely.

We will ask some questions to check we are dealing with the right person and to protect you against fraud. We will then check this information against our records.

Each time you sign in we will send a new security code to your mobile phone.
