Universal Credit

Manage your benefit, payment or claim online

Sign in to your Universal Credit account (opens in new tab or window)

Call the Universal Credit helpline if:

  • you cannot make an ‘new style’ Employment and Support Allowance application online
  • you’re an appointee for someone
  • you cannot use digital services at all - this might be because of disability or your circumstances
  • you have a question about your Universal Credit claim and cannot access your online claim

Universal Credit helpline

  • Telephone: 0800 328 5644 (choose option 3)
  • Textphone: 0800 328 1344 (choose option 3)
  • Welsh language telephone: 0800 328 1744
  • Reply UK(if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 328 5644
