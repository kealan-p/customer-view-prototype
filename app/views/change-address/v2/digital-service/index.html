Manage your benefits

If you get benefit payments, you can use this service to:

  • prove to someone that you get benefits
  • view your personal details
  • share your documents with us

If you get Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) or Carer’s Allowance payments, you can use this service to:

  • view your next benefit payment
  • view your benefit payment history

If you get Universal Credit or Child Maintenance

There’s a different service to:

  • sign in to your Universal Credit account
  • sign in to your Child Maintenance Service account

What you’ll need

If you're signing in for the first time, you'll need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone

To keep your information secure, we will ask you security questions to prove your identity. We may ask for details about your:

  • UK passport
  • payslip or P60
  • Self Assessment
  • tax credits
  • credit file (such as loans, credit cards or mortgages)
